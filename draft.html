From Pasture to Your Door

How It Works.

01

The Farm & Cows

Pasture-raised A2 Jersey cows. Nothing else.

Our cows graze on hundreds of metres of fresh open pasture every single day — 100% grass-fed and grass-finished. No grain, no hormones, no antibiotics, no vaccines. Pure organic Jersey A2 cows selected specifically for the quality and creaminess of their milk. This is how cattle have always been raised, before industrial farming got involved.

02

Daily Health Checks & Sanitation

Every cow. Every day. No exceptions.

Every single cow is tested daily for somatic cell counts, inflammation, and any signs of illness before she is milked. If anything is off, she doesn’t get milked — full stop. Not one customer’s pet has ever gotten sick from our milk. All milking equipment — stainless steel buckets and tanks — is fully cleaned and sanitised before and after every single use. The hygiene standards we hold ourselves to are higher than anything you’ll find at a commercial dairy.

03

Milking & Refrigeration

Straight into stainless steel. Refrigerated to exactly 4°C.

Milk goes directly from the cow into stainless steel buckets, then into stainless steel holding tanks where it is immediately refrigerated to 4°C — not colder. This is critical. Milk refrigerated too cold — below 4°C — begins to freeze and crystallise, destroying the natural enzymes, live bacteria, IGF-1, and bioactive proteins that make raw milk valuable. Too cold is just as damaging as pasteurisation. We follow Aajonus Vonderplanitz’s raw milk cold-chain methodology precisely. No plastic touches the milk at any point.

04

Why Not Pasteurised?

Pasteurised milk is nutritionally dead.

Commercial pasteurised milk is boiled at high heat, which destroys up to 100% of enzymes, 100% of probiotics, 59% of immunoglobulins, and significantly reduces vitamins A, C, E, iron, zinc, calcium and B-complex vitamins. What’s left is watery, nutritionally hollow liquid that actively contributes to digestive issues, inflammation and poor health. You are paying for dead nutrients. Our raw milk is never boiled, never frozen, never processed — every nutrient, every enzyme, every good bacteria arrives at your door alive and intact.

Delivery Day — Tuesday & Wednesday

Milked that morning. At your door within hours.

On delivery days, we milk the cows fresh in the morning, transfer directly to refrigerated stainless steel tanks, load into a refrigerated van maintained at 4°C, and drive straight to you. Your milk arrives within a few hours of being milked. It has never sat on a supermarket shelf. It has never been frozen. No microplastic contact at any point. You are getting the freshest possible raw milk — in 2L glass bottles with stainless steel lids — delivered direct from our farm.

06

At Your Door — Including When You’re Not Home

A free insulated cooler box. Every monthly subscriber.

Every monthly subscription includes a free insulated cooler box. If you’re not home when we deliver, we place your milk inside the cooler box with frozen gel packs and leave it in a safe spot. Your milk stays cold at 4°C until you’re back. For your next delivery: freeze the gel packs the night before, put them back in the cooler box along with your empty glass bottles, and leave it outside on delivery day. We collect your empties, sanitise the bottles, and bring fresh milk. Simple. No waste. No plastic.
