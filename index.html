Raw cow's milk sold for human consumption is illegal in Australia. Our product is sold strictly for pet use only and labelled accordingly.

We use a strict cold-chain process from the dairy to your door. Milk is collected fresh, immediately refrigerated, packed with premium cold gel packs, and delivered in an insulated container. Our glass bottles are sanitised between each delivery cycle.
Plastic leaches microplastics and BPA/BPS into liquids — especially fats like milk. We use 2L glass bottles with stainless steel lids so your milk never touches plastic at any point.
Our minimum subscription is 20L per month with weekly delivery. We offer plans from 20L up to 150L per month, plus larger custom bulk orders.
