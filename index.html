FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about raw milk, delivery, and your subscription.

Our milk comes from 100% pasture-raised grass-fed Australian dairy Jersey cows, selected for premium milk quality and consistency.
Raw cow's milk sold for human consumption is illegal in Australia. Our product is sold for pet use only and labelled accordingly.
Pasteurisation uses heat to extend shelf life, but destroys delicate proteins, enzymes, bacteria and other bioactive compounds. Raw milk preserves all of these naturally.
We use a strict cold-chain process from the dairy to your door. Milk is collected fresh, immediately refrigerated, packed with cold gel packs, and delivered in an insulated container. Glass bottles are sanitised between each delivery cycle.
Plastic leaches microplastics and BPA/BPS into liquids — especially fats like milk. We use 2L glass bottles with stainless steel lids so your milk never touches plastic at any point.
Our minimum subscription is 20L per month with weekly delivery. We also offer one-time trial orders from 5L for those who want to try before subscribing.
