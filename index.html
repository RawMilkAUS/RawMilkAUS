Research & brand philosophy

This section is intentionally written in a neutral way. I did not add unsupported disease, immunity, growth, enzyme or “raw milk cures everything” claims. If you want a research section on the live site, the safest approach is to cite source material accurately, distinguish advocacy from evidence, and have any pet-feeding claims checked by a qualified veterinarian and lawyer before publishing.

What this section can say safely

You can talk about your sourcing philosophy, weekly cold-chain handling, subscription consistency, farm-direct branding, and the fact that some pet owners prefer minimally processed feeding approaches.

What should be reviewed first

Direct claims about height, growth outcomes, immunity, disease prevention, gut repair, enzymes or other health effects should be reviewed carefully before they go on a public sales page.

How to make the site go live

This is the exact GitHub Pages flow. Once you do these steps, the site publishes automatically.

1. Upload the right files

Create or upload index.html in the root of the repository. Upload your logo image as logo.png in the same root folder.

2. Turn on Pages

Open Settings → Pages, choose Deploy from branch, select main, choose /(root), then save.

3. Open the live URL

Your site should publish at https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/RawMilkAUS. GitHub normally takes under a minute to deploy updates.
