How to Care for Your Milk

🌡️

Keep It Cold

Refrigerate immediately on arrival. Keep below 4°C at all times. Raw milk is living food — treat it like fresh produce.

🥛

Shake Before Use

Raw milk is not homogenised — cream rises to the top. Gently invert the bottle a few times before pouring.

📅

Best Within 7–10 Days

Consume within 7–10 days of delivery for peak freshness. Your weekly delivery ensures you always have the freshest possible milk.

♻️

Return Your Bottles

Leave your empty glass bottles out on your next delivery day. Our driver will collect them, sanitise them, and reuse them.
